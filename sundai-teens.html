Sundai Teens

Our Mission

Welcome to Sundai Teens, the youth branch of the Sundai Foundation.


Sundai Teens is a space created by teens, for teens — where high school students can come together to learn coding, build creative projects, and connect with each other. Whether you're a beginner just curious about tech or someone who already loves programming, this is a place to explore, grow, and vibe with other like-minded teens.

What We Do

  • Learn to Code Hands-on sessions where you'll learn the basics of web development, Python, and other creative coding tools.
  • Build Projects Work on fun, real-world projects that make an impact in your community.
  • Join the Club Get involved in the wider Sundai community and see how your skills can help build something bigger.
  • Connect & Collaborate Meet other high school students who share your interests in coding, design, and making things happen.

Why Join Sundai Teens?

Because coding isn't just about sitting behind a screen. It's about creating, problem-solving, and working with friends to build something new. At Sundai Teens, you'll pick up valuable skills, but you'll also find a community that encourages creativity, teamwork, and fun.

Sundai Teens Branches

Guilderland High School Sundai Teens Branch

Sundai Teens began at Guilderland High School, founded by Daniel Joseph. After joining the Sundai club and discovering a passion for coding and community, he wanted to bring that same experience to his school—sparking the start of Sundai Teens.
